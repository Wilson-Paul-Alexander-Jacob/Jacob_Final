We have a lower RV hookup for power and water. We are not sure how hard it is to get a RV up the hill but it is possible.

So if you are ready, and you have made your resevertion, Then come on down. The directions are:

  1. Off old 97 going north, turn left onto Chillowist Road
  2. Follow Chillowist Road for 5.4 Miles
  3. After 5.4 miles, you will come to a "T" in the Road with a street sign with star road on it. Take a right onto Chillowist Road
  4. Drive 1.3 Miles. On the right side, there will be a road with a white sign that says Landers Road. Turn Right
  5. Keep following, until you see a fork in the road. (76 sign for neighbors), keep left for 1 mile.
  6. You will see a fence with a sign for Retreat Drive. Turn Left before Sign.
  7. Follow .10 Miles until Fork. Stay Right.
  8. Follow staright to our propety. There will be a small barn like bulding to your left, keep going around the curves to the propety.
